A career across the data evolution

I have watched data move from a technical task to a participant in how we think.

Foundations

Programming & relational data

When meaning had to be constructed through code, files, schemas and carefully written queries.

Scale

Warehousing, ETL & BI

As organisations learned to consolidate information and turn historical data into shared reporting.

Transformation

SAP & in-memory systems

When SAP HANA changed expectations around speed, integration and real-time enterprise insight.

Convergence

Fabric, cloud & AI

Where data engineering, analytics and intelligence increasingly operate as one connected environment.

For years, we built systems to process data. Now we are building systems that participate in how we think.
